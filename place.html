My Favorite Place

New York City

The bustling streets, the rows of grand skyscrappers,the feeling that you have unlimited potential in the center of the world, all in none other than the never-resting livelihood of New York City. This is the over-romantizced city that remains the dreams of many. I love the diverse cultures in New York and how much it embodies a "mixing pot" of ethnic cusines. I hold dear the feeling of being completely surrouded by crowds yet knowing completely that you are free of judgement.
